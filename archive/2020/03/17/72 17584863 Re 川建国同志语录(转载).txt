January 22: “We have it totally under control. It’s one person coming in from China. It’s going to be just fine.”
我们将疫情完全控制住了，只有一个从中国来的（新冠患者），没问题的。
February 24: “The Coronavirus is very much under control in the USA… Stock Market starting to look very good to me!”
新冠病毒在美国控制得很好，股市看起来不错哦！
February 25: “CDC and my Administration are doing a GREAT job of handling Coronavirus.”
疾控中心和我的政府在处理新冠病毒中做的非常好！
February 25: “I think that's a problem that’s going to go away… They have studied it. They know very much. In fact, we’re very close to a vaccine.”
我认为新冠病毒是一个正在消失的问题。他们（大概是指疾控中心之类的）已经研究过了，他们了解的非常多。事实上，我们很快就会有疫苗了。

美国的15例新冠会在一两天内减到0.
February 26: “We're going very substantially down, not up.”
新冠的病例正在大大减少，而不是增加。
February 27: “One day it’s like a miracle, it will disappear.”
某一天，（新冠）将会奇迹一般消失。
February 28: “We're ordering a lot of supplies. We're ordering a lot of, uh, elements that frankly we wouldn't be ordering unless it was something like this. But we're ordering a lot of different elements of medical.”
我们在订购很多补给品，我们在订购很多，额，我们本来并不需要的物资。但是我们在订购很多医疗物资。
March 2: “You take a solid flu vaccine, you don't think that could have an impact, or much of an impact, on corona?”
你拿一个牛逼的流感疫苗，你不觉得这个牛逼的流感疫苗会对新冠病毒有效吗？
March 2: “A lot of things are happening, a lot of very exciting things are happening and they’re happening very rapidly.”
很多事情正在发生（指控制疫情的措施），很多激动人心的事情正在发生，并且发生的很快。
March 4: “If we have thousands or hundreds of thousands of people that get better just by, you know, sitting around and even going to work — some of them go to work, but they get better.”
如果我们有几千或者几十万（感染新冠病毒的）人只要坐着不动或者甚至去上班就能好转。

我从没有说过感觉自己有病的人应该去上班。
March 5: “The United States… has, as of now, only 129 cases… and 11 deaths. We are working very hard to keep these numbers as low as possible!”
美国，现在只有129例确诊和11死亡。我们在努力让这个数字保持越低越好！

我认为我们在控制疫情方面做得非常好，非常非常好。

翻译： no one knows covid-19 better than me.

我不想让感染者的数量因为一条船而翻倍，这艘船也不是我们的错。（指grand princess cruise，川建国说过这句话之后grand princess cruise上的确诊数据再也没有发布过）
